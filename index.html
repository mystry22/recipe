Recipe Name: Indomie And Chicken

About Indomie

Transcending across all age groups, the brand has managed to win over the hearts and taste palates of both the young and the old.

1 Indomie 2 2 Super Pack
2 Chili 2 Come's with the Indomie

5 Chicken 1/2 Kilo Frozen Chicken wings

Cooking Procedure

  1. Cook the chicken with chicken season
  2. Fry the chicken after booking
  3. Add suitable water quantity to the empty pot and allow to boil till 100oc
  4. Add the noodles and allow to cook for two minutes
  5. Seive with a mesh
  6. Cut your Onions, Fresh Tomatoes and Pepper
  7. Fry all the items in number 6 above
  8. Add chili and the water from the meat to the pot
  9. Add the indomie and allow to cook till water dry
  10. Turn mixture to ensure even distribution of all materials
  11. Serve on a flat plate with chicken beside
